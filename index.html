Research

My research sits at the intersection of robustness and modularity in autonomous aerospace robotics. While I don't claim to be an applied mathematician, I'm deeply drawn to the algorithmic side of things-especially when it comes to making autonomy more reliable, resilient, and fault-tolerant in practice. I enjoy bridging the gap between theory and practice, and I'm just as comfortable tuning parameters in simulation as I am wiring up sensors and running tests on hardware. For me, autonomy isn't just about elegant code-it's about making things actually work, even when they shouldn't.
